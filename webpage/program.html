Our Mancala Project Program

The Graphical User Interface

When you start the program, the first thing you will see is the board, the human side and the computer side. You will also notice that they are greyed out. Only after choosing who will go first can you actually play the game. Whoever's turn it is will have its side unlocked so that the player can move stones on its side.

The Menubar

The Menubar has two menus, File and Settings. The File menu very simply holds the Exit item, which will close the program. The Settings menu has the three different settings for the computer player. You can pick the strategy that you want the computer to use from here.
